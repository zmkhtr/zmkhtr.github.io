Key Features

App Blocking with Quran Verses

Blocks distracting apps and requires reading an Ayah before access, helping maintain focus while ensuring Quran reading progress.

Multilingual Support

Supports English and Indonesian languages with translations and transliterations, adapting to your device settings.

Interactive Quran Reading

Features audio recitation, adjustable Arabic text settings, and translation options for an enhanced reading experience.

Progress Tracking

Tracks your Khatm progress, remembers your last read verse, and allows temporary app unlocking with customizable durations.
