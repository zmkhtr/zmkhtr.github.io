Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

At Khatm, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our mobile application. We want to emphasize that we do not collect or store any of your data - everything is stored locally on your device.

Information We Collect

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Reading Progress: Your Quran reading progress is saved locally on your device
  • App Settings: Your app blocking settings and feature preferences are stored on your device
  • Language Preferences: Your selected language preference is saved locally for translations and content

How Your Data is Used

Your locally stored data is used to:

  • Save your Quran reading progress
  • Remember your app blocking preferences
  • Maintain your selected settings and preferences
  • Provide a personalized reading experience

Data Storage

All your data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers. Your privacy is maintained as all data remains under your control on your device.

App Permissions

Khatm requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Usage Access: To monitor and block specified apps
  • Storage: To save your reading progress and preferences
  • Internet: To download Quran content and translations

Your Data Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access your data directly on your device
  • Clear app data through your device's settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data
  • No external data collection to opt out from

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
